We were sitting on the beach. The waves were a soft background to the moment, a cold drink in my hand, the weather was perfect - I never want to leave this moment. Water and foam called us, we answered. Peace is a rare thing, take advantage of the moment. We swam until the waves pulled us up, maybe it was dangerous. All the sudden, I couldn't see her. She was somewhere else, I'd lost her... Where is she? Where was she?
